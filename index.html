Welcome! I am Fan Feng, a researcher at Vanderbilt University Medical Center, where I explore the intersection of computational biology, imaging, and machine learning to better understand pancreatic islets and diabetes. My work focuses on extracting meaningful insights from complex multimodal datasets using cutting-edge techniques like multiplexed imaging, spatial analysis, and deep learning. Beyond the lab, I am an travel enthusiast and lifelong learner, always curious about new ideas, cultures, and technologies. This site is a space where I share my research, interests, and ongoing journey through science and the world.
